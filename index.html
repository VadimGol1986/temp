JOBS IN HEALTH RESEARCH

Thinking about a career in medicine, health care, or public health?

Looking for a meaningful job in which you will gain skills and work on societal health problems?

Join our team of health researchers in the Montefiore-Einstein Division of General Internal Medicine (DGIM) in the Bronx, New York!

We are seeking motivated college graduates to work as study coordinators on research studies about addiction, HIV, Hepatitis C, incarceration, LGBTQIA+ health, and more.

Staff receive:

  • Hands-on experience conducting clinical research with national and international experts
  • Skills and experience in:
    • Surveys, interviews, and other types of data collection
    • Software and methods for data collection and analysis
    • Scientific writing and presenting at meetings and conferences
  • Being part of a research team

The culture of DGIM values:

  • People who are mission driven with a common goal of improving health, particularly of marginalized communities
  • LGBTQIA+ inclusivity
  • Neurodiversity and unique ways of thinking
  • Bringing intersecting identities to work
  • Sharing fresh ideas to drive change to improve the health of the people we serve
  • The ongoing work of improving diversity, equity, and inclusion
  • Continuous, supportive feedback to learn and grow

While each role is slightly different, study coordinator responsibilities often include:

  • Speaking with potential study participants to engage them in research
  • Conducting visits with participants to complete surveys on health and other behavior
  • Problem-solving day to day challenges and offering suggestions to improve research implementation

To apply, please send a cover letter explaining your interest and experience, along with your resume to Megan Ghiroli at mghiroli@montefiore.org.
